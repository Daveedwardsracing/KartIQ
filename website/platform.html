Platform overview

Built to support every stage of the performance engineering workflow.

RaceIQ is designed to help teams move from raw telemetry files to confident decisions without losing time between sessions. It gives engineers, coaches, and managers a shared view of performance.

The platform is especially valuable in environments where data quality is high but time is limited, because it helps teams turn technical information into operational clarity.

Telemetry review

Upload session data, compare laps, inspect trends, and highlight the corners or phases of a lap that matter most. RaceIQ is aimed at making fast analysis feel routine instead of rushed.

The goal is not just to show traces, but to make the session easier to interpret under real time pressure.

Driver comparisons

Put drivers side by side to understand where time is being won or lost. Use the platform to support coaching discussions with data that is easy to explain.

This helps turn high-detail engineering observations into coaching points that can actually be used on the next run.

Reporting and debriefs

Create clear outputs for internal debriefs or customer-facing reviews. The goal is to help teams communicate technical findings in a more structured way.

Better reporting also improves internal alignment, especially when drivers, engineers, and managers need the same picture presented in different levels of detail.

Team operations

Keep analysis history, notes, planning views, and session outputs in one place so the whole team can work from the same information.

That consistency matters when teams want to build better habits across a season rather than relying on individual workarounds.
