How to position the monthly plan

Present the monthly option as the easiest way for teams to get started, test the workflow, and evaluate RaceIQ during active sessions.

It is especially useful when you want to reduce decision friction for first customers who are curious but not ready to commit long term.

How to position the annual plan

Present the annual option as the operational choice for teams that already know they want a stable analysis platform through the season.

It creates a stronger commercial anchor and helps RaceIQ feel more embedded into a team’s day-to-day process.

Future upgrade path

Later, you can add team, enterprise, white-label, or consultancy packages without changing the core story of the site.

That means today’s pricing can be useful now without locking the business into one permanent commercial model.

Commercial confidence

Even with example pricing, the site now reads like a real product business rather than an internal-only software tool.

That matters because pricing pages shape trust as much as they communicate cost.
